## Introduction

Welcome to Mouseless App! This innovative application allows you to use your webcam to detect hand movements and control your computer without the need for a physical mouse. Mouseless offers a convenient and hands-free alternative for those who may have difficulty using a traditional mouse or simply prefer a more interactive way to interact with their computers.

## Features

- **Hand Detection**: Mouseless utilizes advanced computer vision algorithms to detect the position of your hand accurately.

- **Cursor Control**: Control your computer's cursor with the movement of your hand, providing a mouse-like experience without the need for any physical device.

- **Click and Drag**: Perform left-click and drag actions using intuitive hand gestures.

- **Gesture Customization**: Customize hand gestures to map specific actions, providing a personalized user experience.

- **User-friendly Interface**: The app features a simple and intuitive interface, making it easy for users of all ages and technical backgrounds to get started quickly.

- **Low Resource Usage**: Mouseless is optimized for performance and consumes minimal system resources, ensuring smooth operation even on modest hardware configurations.

## System Requirements

Before installing Mouseless, ensure your computer meets the following minimum requirements:

- **Operating System**: Windows 10, macOS 10.12, or Linux (Ubuntu 18.04+).
- **Webcam**: A built-in or external webcam capable of at least 720p resolution.
- **Processor**: Intel Core i3 or equivalent AMD processor.
- **RAM**: 4GB or higher.
- **Free Disk Space**: 100MB.

## Installation

1. Download the Mouseless installer from the official website at [www.mouselessapp.com](https://www.mouselessapp.com).

2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions to install Mouseless on your computer.

3. Once the installation is complete, launch Mouseless from the Start menu (Windows), Applications folder (macOS), or application menu (Linux).

## How to Use

1. Ensure your webcam is connected and functioning correctly.

2. Launch Mouseless from your applications list.

3. Upon starting the app, it will automatically calibrate and detect your hand's position.

4. Use your hand movements to control the cursor on the screen. Move your hand up, down, left, or right to move the cursor accordingly.

5. Perform a pinch gesture to simulate a left-click, and use the same gesture while dragging your hand to perform a click-and-drag action.

6. Customize gestures by accessing the settings menu and assigning specific actions to different hand movements.

7. To exit Mouseless, simply close the application or use the keyboard shortcut specified in the settings.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using Mouseless, consider the following troubleshooting steps:

- Ensure your webcam is not obstructed and has proper lighting conditions.

- Calibrate the app again by restarting it or accessing the calibration option in the settings.

- Check for software updates for Mouseless and install the latest version.

- If the app is unresponsive, restart it or restart your computer if necessary.

If the problem persists, visit our [support page](https://www.mouselessapp.com/support) for more information or to contact our support team.
